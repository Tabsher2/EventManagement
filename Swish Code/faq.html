Frequently Asked Questions

Doesn't the second player normally have to successfully make the first player's shot before getting to make their own?
In the normal rules of H-O-R-S-E, yes. However, we believed it would be more fun for both players if they each got to take turns making shots. That way if one player is much better than the other, they can still have a good time getting to make their own creative shots.

Isn't H-O-R-S-E normally allowed to have more than two players?
Normally, yes. But in the interest of maximizing fun, we believed it would be more enjoyable if the game was only between two players. A common part of mobile games is waiting for the opponent to take their turn since we all live busy lives. If you were in a game with five players, you might have to wait a full week before it was finally your turn again. Limiting matches to only two players will increase how often you get to play and hopefully make it more fun for all.

Are there microtransactions?
There are microtransactions that allows users to buy ingame currency, but everything in store is attainable without microtransactions.

Do I have to matchmake against an opponent to play everytime?
To play a regular game and recieve currency/score you have to play against a human opponent through matchmaking, but you can play alone in practice mode.

How much time is there between turns and what happens if I miss a turn?
The maximum time allowed between turns is 48 hours. If you miss your turn, you will automatically recieve a letter and your turn will be skipped.

What devices can I play Swish on?
Swish is supported on iOS and Android devices.

Do different ball themes affect how the shot behaves?
No, ball themes are purely cosmetic.
