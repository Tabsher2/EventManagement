Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a University to the Website?
If you are registered as a University on our website, you are able to register one University to the system. Once a University is registered it can start allowing students and RSOs to list it as their University. You can also edit any info about the University you added to the system.

If I register as a University, can I still perform the roles of a student?
No, you cannot register as both a student and a University with only one account. If you would like to perform the roles of both you will need multiple .edu e-mail addresses.

Who is the admin for an RSO?
Whichever user creates the RSO will be assigned as the admin for that RSO.

How do I register a new RSO?
Any student can register a new RSO. However you will need at least 5 other students from the same University to list as well when creating a new RSO otherwise the system will reject the request, as we do not want clubs with less than 6 members.

Who approves my request to join an RSO?
Once you submit a request to join an RSO, a notification will be sent to that admin containing your information. The next time they log on to the system they will see your request on their main page and they can either confirm or deny your membership request. Upon being a confirmed member you will be able to see the events that RSO has published.
